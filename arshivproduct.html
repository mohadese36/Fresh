Classic Beef Sausage

The Classic Beef Sausage is a premium product made from the finest cuts of beef. We take pride in crafting this sausage with traditional recipes that have been passed down through generations. The beef is carefully selected, seasoned with a blend of natural spices, and encased in a natural casing for the perfect bite.

Whether you're grilling it for a family BBQ, adding it to your favorite pasta, or enjoying it in a sandwich, our Classic Beef Sausage is versatile and perfect for any occasion. With its rich flavor and juicy texture, it’s sure to satisfy all meat lovers.

Ingredients

Our Classic Beef Sausage contains the following ingredients:

Beef (70%)
Pork fat (20%)
Salt
Black pepper
Garlic powder
Onion powder
Paprika
Natural casing (sheep or hog casing)
Water
Natural preservatives (celery powder)

Our sausages do not contain any artificial additives, preservatives, or MSG. We only use the best quality natural spices to enhance the taste of the beef.

Storage Conditions

To ensure the best quality and taste, we recommend the following storage conditions for our Classic Beef Sausage:

  • Keep refrigerated at 4°C (39°F) or below.
  • If you do not plan to use the sausage within a week, we suggest freezing it for up to 3 months.
  • For frozen sausages, ensure that they are thawed properly in the refrigerator before cooking.
  • Once opened, consume within 3 days for optimal flavor and freshness.

Always check the expiration date on the package for safety. Do not leave the sausage out at room temperature for extended periods.
